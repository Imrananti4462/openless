## What is openless?

Openless acts as a bridge between your voice and your computer. It listens to what you say and turns it into clean, polished text. You press a key, speak your thoughts, and release. The application inserts the result directly into your current program. You do not need to copy or paste anything. This tool functions inside web browsers, word processors, and email clients.

## ⚙️ System Requirements

Openless runs on modern versions of Windows. We suggest Windows 10 or Windows 11 for the best experience. The application requires a stable internet connection to process your speech through artificial intelligence. Ensure your computer has a working microphone. Wired headsets usually provide better accuracy than built-in laptop microphones.

## 🚀 Getting Started

Launch the app from your desktop shortcut or the Start menu. It will remain in your system tray—the area near your clock—while it runs.

1. Locate the Openless icon in your system tray.
2. Open the settings menu to set your preferred microphone.
3. Choose a hotkey that feels comfortable for your hand. This is the key you hold down while you speak.
4. Click a text box in your web browser or note app.
5. Press and hold your hotkey, state your sentence clearly, then release.
6. Watch as the text appears on your screen seconds later.

## 🧠 Behind the Scenes

This tool uses advanced speech recognition models to capture your words. It then sends this data to a language model to fix grammar and punctuation. This process happens in real-time. The goal is to remove the effort of typing. You speak naturally, and the app handles the formatting. This makes writing emails or long reports much faster.

## 🛠️ Customizing Your Experience

You can change how the software works in the settings menu. Adjust the noise threshold if the app picks up background sounds too often. You can also change the prompt style. This tells the AI if you want your text to sound professional, casual, or concise. Select the mode that fits your specific needs.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the app store my recordings?**
The app focuses on processing your voice into text. It does not save private audio clips to your drive.

**Can I use this with professional software?**
Yes. Since the app types directly at your cursor, it works in any software that accepts text input.

**Why does the text take a moment to appear?**
The app sends your voice to a server to use deep learning for high accuracy. This adds a slight delay. Faster internet speeds provide faster results.

**How do I close the application?**
Right-click the icon in your system tray and select Exit. You can also restart your computer to stop the program.

## 💡 Pro Tips for Better Accuracy

Speak at a normal pace. You do not need to shout. Enunciate your words clearly for better recognition. If you work in a quiet space, the tool will perform better. Keep your microphone at a consistent distance from your mouth. Using a noise-canceling microphone helps if you work in an office with other people.

## 📝 Troubleshooting

If you do not see text, check your microphone connection. Ensure you pressed the hotkey for the entire time you spoke. If the app stops working, click the icon in your system tray and select Restart. Check if you have an active internet connection. If the issue keeps happening, restart your computer. You can check the help logs in the settings menu if you need to troubleshoot further.
